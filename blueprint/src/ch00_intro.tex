\chapter*{Introduction / Notation}

The numbering in part~I follows Diestel, \emph{Graph Theory}, 5th edition. In
particular we will mostly use the same notation.

\begin{description}
  \item[{$[X]^{<\omega}$}] the set of all finite subsets of $X$
  \item[$IX$] an inflation of $X$: $Y$ is an $IX$ iff $X$ is a contraction minor
    of $Y$
\end{description}
